This project analyzes housing price trends, growth patterns, and affordability across cities in Montgomery County, Maryland using Zillow and U.S. Census data.

The goal is to evaluate real estate markets from an investment perspective, identifying areas with strong appreciation potential, manageable risk, and varying levels of affordability.

Overview


The analysis is framed from an investment perspective, focusing on identifying markets with strong appreciation potential, understanding risk through volatility, and evaluating affordability relative to income.

Objectives
Analyze housing price trends over time
Identify high growth markets
Measure price volatility (risk)
Evaluate housing affordability using income data
Explore relationships between population, income, and home prices
